# CAT & ONION presskit

![](/cat-and-onion-presskit/logos/animated_logo.gif)

## Description[#](#description)

CAT & ONION is a short cat adventure packed with curious characters, strange environments and good vibes. Climb, talk, fly and smack your way to places where no cat has ever gone before!

## Release dates and platforms[#](#release-dates-and-platforms)

January 28, 2024 - [Itch.io](https://zylinski.itch.io/cat-and-onion) - Windows, Mac and Linux

March 12, 2024 - [Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2781210/CAT__ONION/) - Windows, Mac, Steam Deck and Linux

## Price[#](#price)

* $3.49
* €3.49
* £2.97

## Languages[#](#languages)

English

## Developer[#](#developer)

Solo indie game developer Karl Zylinski. Karl has made all the code, graphics, music and sound for CAT & ONION. Based in Sweden.

Marketing by Karl Zylinski and Geraldine Lee.
